Writer(s): TYLER JOSEPH
This song was recorded with Tim Anderson at Werewolf Heart in Los Angeles, California.
Critic Jason Pettigrew wrote a review of this song's album for Sputnikmusic and Alternative Press and praised the band's blend of genres in their songs, e.g. on this one, "Message Man".
The line "Please use discretion when you're messing with the message, man" can be interpreted in different way when written without a comma. With comma it means that the singer addresses the listener ("man") to be careful with what they listen to ("the message"). Without comma the singer mentions himself, the message man.
