Writer(s): TAYLOR SWIFT, ED SHEERAN
The sixth single from the album released on the 16th of July 2013. On the 18th of October 2012, the song was leaked online.
This song is a guitar ballad written by Taylor Swift and Ed Sheeran in her backyard.
Taylor Swift explained the song's meaning by saying, "Everything looks different to you because of this one person, because one new person came into your life. It's really cool."
This track was certified gold by the Recorded Music NZ, Recording Industry Association of America, and double platinum by the Australian Recording Industry Association.
Taylor Swift and Ed Sheeran performed "Everything Has Changed" on Britain's Got Talent on the 8th of June 2013.
The music video shows two kids, a boy and a girl, who look like the younger versions of Taylor Swift and Ed Sheeran. They are at school but their activities are different from those of the other children.
