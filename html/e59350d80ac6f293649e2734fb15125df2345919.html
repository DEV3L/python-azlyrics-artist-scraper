Writer(s): TAYLOR SWIFT
This song took an inspiration from Taylor Swift's supposed relationship with John Mayer.
Guitar slurring, added to the background instrumentals, is a playing technique John Mayer uses very frequently.
John Mayer's 2013 song "Paper Doll" contains a line "And if those angel wings don't fly, someone's going to paint you another sky" which may be an answer to the Taylor Swift's here line, "You paint me a blue sky".
When Taylor Swift appeared on the episode of The Ellen DeGeneres Show on the 1st of November 2010, Ellen asked Taylor directly if this song is about John Mayer. Swift replied, "I'm still going to continue to write about my life and I guess people are going to continue to speculate about it, and I'm going to continue to never tell them what the song's about."
This track has been never released as a single, but it still could chart on the US Billboard Hot 100 and the Canadian Hot 100.
