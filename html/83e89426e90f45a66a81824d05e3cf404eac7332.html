Writer(s): Tyler Joseph, Josh Dun
The first verse of these lyrics describes Tyler Joseph's fear of Blurryface, his alter ego made of depression and insecurities. Blurryface is somewhere in the back of Joseph's mind, driving him insane.
The judge of this song is apparently God from Christianity since both members of the band are known to be Christians.
"The Judge" was recorded with producer Mike Crossey at Livingston Studios in London.
The words "Josh Dun" can be heard at 4:25 point in this song, which was explained by Tyler Joseph on Twitter, "if you listen close 'the judge' at 4:25, i yell 'josh dun'. he was standing on the other side of the glass when i recorded that take."
