Writer(s): TAYLOR SWIFT
Taylor Swift explained on her website that this song was a "love song... written to my team".
PopMatters editor Dave Heaton described this track by saying, "It's an anthem of defiance, where the defiant ones are gaining their energy from memories and story-making."
The song was first performed by Taylor Swift at the Royal Caribbean cruise ship Allure of the Sea in January, 2011.
There is a version of this song from the Brazilian edition of Taylor Swift's album "Speak Now World Tour – Live" (2011) with additional Portuguese verses written and performed by Brazilian artist Paula Fernandes.
The music video for the single version of the song features Paula Fernandes singing in a studio whilst Taylor Swift sings at a concert.
