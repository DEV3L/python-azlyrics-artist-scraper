Writer(s): TYLER JOSEPH
"Jumpsuit" is one of the first two singles released on July 11, 2018 to promote Twenty One Pilots' fifth album, "Trench" (2018). The other single is "Nico And The Niners".
Josh Dun explained in an interview with BBC Radio 1 that this song began to evolve from a bass riff Tyler Joseph came up with towards the end of their last tour. He used to play the riff during sound checks before shows.
This track was chosen to be the first single off of the new album following "Blurryface" (2015) since it continues the story. Josh Dun said, "It kind of zooms out a little bit and there is a full narrative. Musically, I'm very excited about it. It continues to be diverse."
"Jumpsuit" is a reference to a yellow jumpsuit Clancy uses to escape the city of Dema. Dema is a fictional walled city ruled by nine bishops, one of which can be seen wearing a red hood in the music video for this song. Clansy is a character who wants Dema to be free from bishops.
Josh Dun elaborated upon a background of this song by saying, "...it is kind of like picking up with where Blurryface ended. So it felt like the first track back to kind of bridge that gap and go from one record to the next."
The official music video was premiered on the 11th of July 2018.
