Writer(s): TAYLOR SWIFT, MAX MARTIN, ED SHEERAN, NAYVADIUS WILBURN, JOHAN SHELLBACK
The album's third single. It was first released to French contemporary pop radio on the 14th of November 2017. It was sent to hit radio in the United States on the 3rd of December 2017.
The music video shows Taylor Swift in various locales: with Future on a yacht in Miami, Florida, with Ed Sheeran at a nightclub in Tokyo, Japan, and with various friends on a double-decker bus in London, England. London scenes show several references to snakes, because of Swift's "snake" reputation.
Taylor Swift and Ed Sheeran performed "End Game" for the first time during the iHeartRadio Jingle Ball on the 2nd of December 2017.
The music video for "...Ready For It?" shows graffiti with Chinese characters, two sets of which are translated as "The Future" and "Eddie".
