This song was written by Tyler Joseph for this wife Jenna Joseph. He describes how he would fight for her.
"I got your six" is a reference to the pilot system where directions correspond to hours on the clock: 12 o'clock means "forward", and 6 o'clock means "behind". This way "I got your six" means "I got your back".
