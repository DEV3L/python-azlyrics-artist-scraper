Writer(s): ROSE LIZ, SWIFT TAYLOR ALISON
Taylor Swift co-produced this song alongside Nathan Chapman.
The second single from her debut album. The song was later included on the international release of her second studio album, "Fearless" (2008).
The song was inspired by Taylor Swift's experience with Drew Hardwick, her classmate. She had feelings for him but he was unaware of that and continually spoke about his girlfriend to Swift. Swift recalled his words, "How beautiful she was, how nice and smart and perfect she was. And I sat there and listened, never meaning it any of the times I said 'Oh, I'm so happy for you.'"
"Teardrops On My Guitar" is Taylor Swift's breakthrough single. Because of it she became popular in the United States.
The single was certified triple platinum by the Recording Industry Association of America.
The music video received a nomination for MTV Video Music Award for Best New Artist. American singer and actor Tyler Hilton portrayed Drew in it.
