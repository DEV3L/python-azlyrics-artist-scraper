This song expresses the protagonist's thoughts and feelings toward a human being's mortality. Death is always somewhere in the future, but he just doesn't know when.
The narrator contemplates about some ways of dealing with death, one of them being to go "around" death by using science and medicine, but it would take a "scientific miracle" to achieve immortality.
The title of this song apparently refers to the fictional city of DEMA, where one can try to escape if disguising himself by wearing yellow in order to get past the walls and bishops that guard them.
Nicholas Bourbaki (Nico) is the name of the infamous Bishop of Dema, introduced by their single "Nico and the Niners". It is also a reference to the name of a group of mathematicians, formed in 1935, which were famous for making an attempt to use mathematics to prove the existence of God.
