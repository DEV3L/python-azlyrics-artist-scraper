Writer(s): tyler joseph
The title of this song means that when a celebrity dies, committing suicide, their name written in neon lights, which symbolizes their success and fame, becomes a kind of their gravestone.
The line "Well, I want you to follow me down to the bottom, underneath the insane asylum" refer to the city of DEMA that can be seen as an insane asylum. In order to escape from DEMA, the banditos use underground tunnels.
Tyler Joseph expresses to the listener that suicide is not the right way to end your suffering because the pain you're trying to escape just will be passed onto your relatives and friends.
The main theme of this song could be a shot at the Netflix Original Series which deals with suicide, "13 Reasons Why". The show was criticized for "dramatizing suicide".
