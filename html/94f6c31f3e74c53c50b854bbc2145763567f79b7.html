Writer(s): Tyler Joseph
This song appears on the original soundtrack to the film "Suicide Squad" (2016).
The music video for "Heathens" was filmed in an actual prison with the actual prisoners.
Tyler Joseph said to Hits 97.3, "As I was writing the song, I was like, 'I want this song to be a Twenty One Pilots song first. I want it to resonate with our fans and make sense at our show.' Even though the movie—the themes of the movie—inspired the beginning of it, as the lyrics came together and as the song came together, I realized this was our song."
With the video, the band wanted to pay tribute to one of the most famous prison concerts of all. Their stylist Amanda Valentine told Billboard magazine, "We were trying to give a very subtle nod to Johnny Cash's Folsom Prison performance. You can barely just make out the bolo tie."
