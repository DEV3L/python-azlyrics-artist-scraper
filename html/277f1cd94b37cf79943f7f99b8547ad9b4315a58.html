Writer(s): TYLER JOSEPH
This song was inspired by a real-life story, when vocalist Tyler Joseph was rushing for a college class and forgot to lock his car, which led to his GPS, radio and CDs being stolen by thieves.
"Car Radio" was originally written and recorded in 2011 for their second studio album, "Regional at Best". It was re-released as the fifth single from their third studio album, "Vessel", in March 2014.
Tyler Joseph noted about this song, "I would say it's one of my favorites, but honestly my favorite song is always the one I'm working onâ€¦ but what I like about it is that there's really no structure to it that is similar to any other song I've heard before, in the sense that there's no chorus. But it makes sense to me."
The music video shows Tyler Joseph performing this song on a concert while wearing a ski mask.
