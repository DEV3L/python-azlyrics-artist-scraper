Writer(s): TAYLOR ALISON SWIFT, IMOGEN JENNIFER HEAP
Taylor Swift collaborated on this song with British singer-songwriter Imogen Heap. She told The Sun, "We did a one-take vocal, and it was just so inspiring to see a woman do all the technical things in the studio that you usually see men do."
The meaning of this track was described by Taylor Swift, "It's about comparing getting over a relationship to sobriety. It has a lot of different references between addiction and moving on from a relationship."
Speaking with Elle magazine, Taylor Swift commented on the song, "'Clean' I wrote as I was walking out of Liberty in London. Someone I used to date—it hit me that I'd been in the same city as him for two weeks and I hadn't thought about it. When it did hit me, it was like, 'Oh, I hope he's doing well.' And nothing else." She realized that she was totally over him.
