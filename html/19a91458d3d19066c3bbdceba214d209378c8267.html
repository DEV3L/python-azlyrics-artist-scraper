Writer(s): MICHAEL SILVERSHER, ALEX ROXWELL, PATRICIA SILVERSHER, JUDY ROFE
Tyler Joseph explained, "I like putting this song first on the album, because it kinda puts the listener through kind of a song structure boot camp, if you will, when it comes to them being able to anticipate what the rest of the album is."
"Ode to Sleep" also appears on the Twenty One Pilots' EP "Three Songs". The second verse was originally written by Tyler Joseph for his solo album "No Phun Intended" in 2007-2008.
A music video released for this song on the 31st of December 2014, was composed of footage captured from three concerts from the past three years.
In 2017, this track was certified "Gold" by the RIAA for over 500'000 copies sold.
