Writer(s): TYLER JOSEPH
Many verses of this song are derived from Tyler Joseph's "Street Poetry".
This song was released as the sixth and final single from their fourth studio album "Blurryface".
The "One time to twist and one time to shout" line references the song "Twist and Shout", originally recorded by The Top Notes and made a hit by The Beatles.
The "Heavydirtysoul" music video, released on the 3rd of February 2017, was the final video of the "Blurryface" era.
