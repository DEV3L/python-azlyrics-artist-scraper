This song delivers a message that people should use their potential to the full extent, because otherwise they live their lives like those birds which have wings but don't use them.
The third verse contains direct references to the Christian God, that he patiently waits for those which still repeat their daily actions without thinking much about the good and the evil, although their souls know better what side in life to choose.
